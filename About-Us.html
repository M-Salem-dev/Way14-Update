Who are Way 14

Founded in Egypt in 2019. We are a full team of engineers composers, designers, photographers, directors, writers and programmers We have a passion for global art production. General technical production in all works. And we develop ourselves every day in order to produce specialized artistic production in all artistic productions to build a better future, photos, videos and music - and access to innovative tools that support the creative process.

We have integrated technologies accumulated over the past years into a comprehensive audio processing platform, providing automation of audio processing processes, helping users in various fields to find long-overlooked information in audio and creating greater value.

We worked to equip the company with the latest technologies according to the latest developments in the world.

We also extend our passion and vision for music to processing other types of sounds. It is currently used in music, smart manufacturing, new media, online education, and other fields.

What We Do?

We aim to be globally competitive. We’re pioneered in providing new and creative ideas and strategic planning for all our clients. We believe that with hard work, nothing is impossible to reach. We always strive to be the leaders in the industry as we work with businesses and non-profits entities to magnify the growing power and market of digital marketing. Helping and working with clients to deliver an increase in leads, brand awareness, growing sales and support other business goals. We do believe that understanding customer’s needs through building long lasting strong relationship with them is the core of any effective communication. We have a great mix of clientele which includes some of the world’s most famous brands and local (Small and medium-sized enterprises) who operate across many sectors. We work with you as a partner, this way we not only deliver you great results. But also foster a lasting relationship, because it is no secret that your success is good for us too.
